Memonymous was created by a group of Grade 8 students who believe that anybody should be able to share their own story for free and anonymously. We understand that this may not be easily possible, but the action that we determined can most easily provide this to the greatest amount of people is to publish this website online.

Memoirs are shared across everyone who visits this site.
